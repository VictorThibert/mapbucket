Post-secondary Institutions and Affiliates in Montreal
2018-03-29
Victor Thibert

This is a map of the land and buildings owned by or affiliated with Montreal's post-secondary institutions. Numerous hospitals are part of the CHUM (University de Montreal) and MUHC (McGill University Health Centre) systems. The green institutions indicate Quebec's Cegep schools. More information can be found here.

Data was collected through OpenStreetMap's layer data. A higher resolution and unlabelled version can be found here.

This map was inspired by Bill Rankin's "College Town" map found on Radical Cartography.
